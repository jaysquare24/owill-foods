About
OWILL FOODS

We are proud of what we do and how we do it.
our core Values are Excellence, Integrity, and Quality

For over 20 years Ayoola Foods Limited has dedicated itself to producing indigenous food products of unbeatable quality, making it a household name for Nigerians all over the world. Incorporated in 1991, Ayoola Foods focused on exporting Nigeria food products to meet the desires of Nigerians in the diaspora. In 2004, we decided to shift our focus to our home country Nigeria, where we now produce indigenous premium quality food products to satisfy our highly esteemed customers, locally and in the diaspora. We have a variety of products which includes Plantain Flour, Rice Flour, Cassava Fufu, Beans Flour, Yam Flour [Amala], Brown Beans, Ayoola Potato and Our Number One best seller the Ayoola Poundo Yam. We have also introduced a Special Pack which is a mix of our products, at an affordable price. The Special Pack is a great option for large families and is also a great gift for loved ones.

OWill POUNDO YAM

Our Poundo Yam is sourced from a carefully selected specie of yam tubers that are rich in vitamins, minerals and fiber. It is specially processed to create the taste and aroma of freshly prepared pounded yam. Our Poundo yam takes as little as 5 minutes to prepare and can be enjoyed with any soup. Ayoola Poundo Yam is made without preservatives and can also be used as a thickening agent for soups and stews. Available in 450grams, 0.9kg, 1.8kg, and 4.5kg packs. It is also available in 9.1kg bags.

OWill YAM FLOUR [AMALA]

Our Yam Flour for Amala is made from a special specie of yam and sourced from carefully selected local suppliers and organic farms. This ensures that our product is free from pesticides and chemicals. It is specially processed to produce our traditional Amala that is very popular in South Western parts of Nigeria. Ayoola Yam Flour (Amala) can be eaten with any soup. Available in 0.9kg and 1.8kg pack sizes.

OWill POTATO

Our Potato Flour is made from 100% Irish potatoes. It is a great addition to your diet because it is gluten-free, nutrient-dense and a source of antioxidants, which fight free radicals that cause diseases in body cells. Ayoola Potato Flour can be used to make a variety of dishes like mashed potatoes, cereals, indigenous swallows and much more. Available in 500grams and 1kg pack sizes.
